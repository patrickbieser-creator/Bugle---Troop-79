Req 1: A Family Expense - (Summary of requirement) Choose an item that your family might want to purchase considered a major expense. Write a plan on how your family will save for expense. Discuss the plan with counselor and family. Develop a written shopping strategy. Determine the quality of the item or service. Find out where you can buy the item. Call around; study ads. Consider alternatives. Can you buy the item used? Should you wait for a sale?

Req 2: A 13 Week Personal Budget - (Summary of Requirement) Prepare and track a 13 week personal budget - income and expenses. Check in periodically with your counselor to show progress.

Req 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10: Read the official Personal Management MB Pamphlet. It's well written and has all information needed. Then reach out to Patrick or Lisa via email, or band, with another adult cc'd, with any questions, or when ready to discuss any of the requirements. Scouts can also catch Patrick or Lisa at Troop meetings. They are also happy to meet via Google Meet or Zoom. Message either to make an appointment!

Lisa Pieper and Patrick Bieser are happy to meet with Scouts who have made progress on this merit badge. They are also available via Google Meet or Zoom most evenings during the week.

Resource: A great resource for tracking progress is this Personal Management Workbook.
