Req 1: A Family Expense - (Summary of requirement) Choose an item that your family might want to purchase considered a major expense. Write a plan on how your family will save for expense. Discuss the plan with counselor and family. Develop a written shopping strategy. Determine the quality of the item or service. Find out where you can buy the item. Call around; study ads. Consider alternatives. Can you buy the item used? Should you wait for a sale?

Req 2: A 13 Week Personal Budget - (Summary of Requirement) Prepare and track a 13 week personal budget - income and expenses. Check in periodically with your counselor to show progress.

Req 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10: Read the official Personal Management MB Pamphlet. It's well written and has all information needed.
